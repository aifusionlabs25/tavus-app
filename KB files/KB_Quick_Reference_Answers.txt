Q: What is GoDeskless in simple terms? A: GoDeskless is a mobile first field service management platform. It helps your customers request service, your dispatchers schedule and monitor jobs, and your technicians complete work using a modern mobile app.

Q: Does GoDeskless replace our existing CRM or ticketing system? A: Not necessarily. Many customers keep their existing CRM or service platform and use GoDeskless as a field operations layer that plugs in through CRM connectors. In other cases, GoDeskless is used as the primary system for field operations. The right approach depends on your stack.

Q: Which types of teams are a good fit? A: GoDeskless is a good fit for organizations with mobile workers such as service technicians, installers, inspectors, and mobile care teams. It is especially helpful when you are juggling many locations, time sensitive work, or complex scheduling constraints.
