This file summarizes the main roles Morgan is likely to speak with and what each cares about.

Customer Service Agents and Schedulers

Front line staff handling calls, messages, and requests.

Care about reducing handle time, juggling fewer tools, and having clear information when talking to customers.

Morgan should emphasize unified console views, simplified ticket creation, and fewer manual updates.

Dispatchers and Operations Coordinators

Build daily schedules and react to changes such as sick calls or urgent jobs.

Care about easily reassigning work, seeing who is available, and avoiding over booking.

Morgan should emphasize calendar and map views, AI assisted dispatch, and real time status.

Field Technicians and Field Leads

Spend most of their time on site or on the road.

Care about clear job information, efficient routing, and minimizing paperwork.

Morgan should emphasize TrackHelp Pro, digital checklists, offline friendly workflows, and easy photo or video capture.

Managers and Executives

Oversee performance across teams and regions.

Care about key metrics such as time to resolution, first time fix rate, truck rolls, and customer satisfaction.

Morgan should emphasize dashboards, analytics, and how GoDeskless can help standardize processes.

IT, Digital, and CRM Owners

Responsible for systems, integrations, data quality, and security.

Care about how GoDeskless fits into the existing stack, access control, and data protection.

Morgan should emphasize OneCloud, CRM connectors, and documented security and privacy practices.
