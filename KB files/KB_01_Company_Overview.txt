GoDeskless is a field service management software company focused on deskless workers: technicians, installers, inspectors, and mobile teams who spend their days in the field rather than at a desk.

The GoDeskless platform combines a web console and mobile tools to help organizations:

Streamline ticket creation and scheduling.

Coordinate customers, dispatchers, and field technicians in real time.

Reduce manual paperwork and disconnected tools.

Improve time to resolution and first time fix rates.

Key ideas:

Mobile first: GoDeskless is built around how field teams actually work on the move.

AI powered: Scheduling, dispatch, and routing use intelligent logic to match the right technician to the right job.
