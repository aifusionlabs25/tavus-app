GoDeskless offers a suite of products that work together as a single field service platform.

Core components:

SmartAgent Console

Web based console for customer service agents, schedulers, and dispatchers.

Central place to triage tickets, schedule jobs, communicate with customers, and coordinate technicians.

TrackHelp Pro App

Mobile app for field technicians and field team leads.

Provides job details, checklists, navigation, and real time communication.

SnapHelp Web App

Browser based experience used by customers.

Allows customers to request service, share photos or video, and receive updates without installing a native app.

GoDeskless OneCloud

AI enabled platform layer that unifies data about customers, technicians, assets, and jobs.

Powers scheduling, routing, analytics, and integration with other systems.

CRM Connectors

Prebuilt integrations that embed GoDeskless capabilities inside popular CRM and service tools.

Provide bi directional sync of tickets, customer data, and field activities.
